How to Play Spelling Bee

Game Rules

This word game is designed to challenge your vocabulary and word-building skills. In this engaging puzzle, players explore the fascinating world of word creation. The format encourages creative thinking and language mastery.

  • Create words using the 7 letters provided in the hexagonal grid
  • Each word must contain the center letter - this is a core rule
  • Words must be at least 4 letters long
  • Letters can be used more than once in a word
  • Common English words only - our dictionary excludes proper nouns

Scoring System

The scoring system rewards both quick thinking and extensive vocabulary. Master the scoring rules to maximize your points and achieve higher ranks.

  • 4-letter words: 1 point
  • Longer words: 1 point per letter
  • Words using all 7 letters (pangrams): +7 bonus points
  • Find all possible words to maximize your score!

Game Mechanics

The Spelling Bee puzzle presents players with seven carefully selected letters arranged in a hexagonal pattern. The unique layout features one central letter that must be included in every word. This distinctive format creates an engaging word-discovery challenge.
