SpellingBee Game Rules

The SpellingBee game is designed to challenge your vocabulary and word-building skills. In this engaging SpellingBee puzzle, players explore the fascinating world of word creation. The SpellingBee format encourages creative thinking and language mastery.

  • Create words using the 7 letters provided in the SpellingBee grid
  • Each word must contain the center letter - this is a core SpellingBee rule
  • Words must be at least 4 letters long in SpellingBee
  • Letters can be used more than once in a SpellingBee word
  • Common English words only - the SpellingBee dictionary excludes proper nouns

SpellingBee Scoring System

The SpellingBee scoring system rewards both quick thinking and extensive vocabulary. Master the SpellingBee scoring rules to maximize your points and achieve higher ranks.

  • 4-letter words: 1 point in SpellingBee scoring
  • Longer words: 1 point per letter in the SpellingBee game
  • Words using all 7 letters (pangrams): +7 bonus points in SpellingBee
  • Find all possible words to maximize your SpellingBee score!
